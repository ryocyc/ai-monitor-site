xAI Launches Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0, Its Most Capable Voice Agent, Now on API

xAI has released grok-voice-think-fast-1.0, a flagship voice model designed for high-stakes, multi-turn workflows in customer support, sales, and enterprise applications.

xAI Blog 2026-04-23 22:25 UTC Key: xai-blog::identity::grok-voice-think-fast-1 Confidence: strong Mode: claude

Article body

The new model is purpose-built for messy, real-world voice interactions: it handles noisy environments, heavy accents, frequent interruptions, and rapid speech across more than 25 supported languages. Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 combines real-time reasoning with no added latency, allowing the model to think through complex queries in the background while maintaining snappy, natural responses. It also prioritizes precise data entry and read-back, able to collect and confirm emails, addresses, phone numbers, and account numbers even when users speak quickly or correct themselves mid-sentence.

The model claims the top position on the τ-voice Bench leaderboard, which tests full-duplex voice agents under realistic conditions including background noise, accents, and turn-taking. xAI says grok-voice-think-fast-1.0 is harder to fool than prior voice models, reasoning through edge cases before responding rather than defaulting to confident-sounding but incorrect answers.

Grok Voice is already live powering Starlink's phone sales and customer support at +1 (888) GO STARLINK. xAI reports a 20% conversion rate on sales calls and a 70% resolution rate on support inquiries handled autonomously with no human in the loop. The agent uses 28 distinct tools across hundreds of workflows, handling high-stakes tasks including hardware troubleshooting, service credits, and hardware replacements.

Why this matters

  • Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 is the first xAI voice model to reach the top spot on a public benchmark designed for realistic, noisy, multi-turn voice scenarios.
  • Real-world deployment at Starlink shows meaningful business outcomes: one in five inbound sales calls converts, and the majority of support calls resolve without human intervention.
  • The combination of real-time reasoning and zero added latency addresses a long-standing trade-off in voice AI between speed and intelligence.
