Mistral AI Pricing Plans

Mistral AI now offers four tiers for Le Chat — a free personal assistant, a $14.99 Pro plan, a $24.99 per-user Team plan, and a custom Enterprise tier.

Mistral Pricing 2026-04-22 23:49 UTC Key: mistral-pricing::identity::mistral-pricing-products-solutions-r Confidence: moderate Mode: claude

Article body

Mistral AI's pricing page shows four distinct tiers anchored by Le Chat, described as a free personal AI assistant for everyday life and work. The free plan grants access to the company's state-of-the-art models, up to 500 saved memories, picture generation, project-based chat organization, and a directory of 40+ enterprise connectors — all without a price tag. It is positioned clearly at the entry point of the lineup.

The Pro plan, priced at $14.99 per month excluding taxes, targets enhanced productivity with higher usage limits across messages, web searches, extended thinking, and deep research reports. It includes up to 15 GB of document storage, up to 1,000 projects, Mistral Vibe for all-day coding with pay-as-you-go beyond quotas, and state-of-the-art image generation. Chat and email support are included. A $0 student plan mirrors the base Pro tier at no charge.

The Team plan costs $24.99 per user per month excluding taxes and adds collaborative and administrative features: up to 30 GB of storage per user, domain name verification, data export, and an admin API. Enterprise deployments are handled as a custom engagement, offering private infrastructure powered by custom models, UI, and tools, with pricing handled via a sales contact. The comparison table confirms that enterprise tiers unlock features such as SAML SSO, white label options, and audit logs.

Why this matters

  • Mistral is now matching the tiered pricing structure of rivals like OpenAI and Anthropic, with a free tier that includes meaningful capabilities rather than a pure trial.
  • The inclusion of Mistral Vibe (coding assistant) and deep research in paid tiers signals Mistral is expanding Le Chat beyond chat into developer-facing workflows.
  • Enterprise deployments with custom models and MCP connectors indicate Mistral is actively courting business customers who need self-hosted or private AI infrastructure.

Source note

  • All pricing details come directly from mistral.ai/pricing. No model names, specific rate limits, or effective dates were shown on the page; the Pro and Team prices are noted as excluding taxes. The free plan's 500-memory cap and the 40+ enterprise connectors were the most concrete feature callouts for the base tier.
