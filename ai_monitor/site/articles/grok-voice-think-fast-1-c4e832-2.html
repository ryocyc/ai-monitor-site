xAI's Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 Brings Advanced Voice Agent Capabilities to API

xAI has released grok-voice-think-fast-1.0, a flagship voice agent model designed for complex real-world workflows including customer support, sales, and enterprise applications.

xAI Blog 2026-04-23 22:25 UTC Key: xai-blog::identity::grok-voice-think-fast-1 Confidence: strong Mode: claude

Article body

xAI has introduced grok-voice-think-fast-1.0, describing it as the company's most capable voice agent to date. The model is built for multi-step, ambiguous workflows in customer support, sales, appointment booking, and restaurant reservations. It was developed in collaboration with Starlink, combining what xAI calls top-tier intelligence with low response latency and organic conversational ability. The company claims the model achieves top ranking on the τ-voice Bench leaderboard, which tests full-duplex voice agents under realistic conditions including background noise, accents, interruptions, and turn-taking. It natively supports 25 or more languages.

The model includes real-time reasoning that runs in the background without adding latency, allowing it to work through complex queries while maintaining natural conversational flow. It is also designed to handle precise data entry tasks—collecting emails, addresses, phone numbers, and other structured data—even when information is spoken quickly or with a strong accent, and it accepts natural corrections. xAI notes the model is harder to fool than typical voice models: it reasons through edge cases before responding rather than defaulting to confident but incorrect answers.

Grok Voice Think Fast is already powering Starlink's phone sales and customer support experience. According to xAI, the system handles a 20% conversion rate on sales inquiries and resolves 70% of customer support cases autonomously with no human intervention. The single agent uses 28 distinct tools across hundreds of workflows, handling tasks from hardware troubleshooting and replacement to service credits. The API is now publicly available.

Why this matters

  • xAI claims the model tops the τ-voice Bench leaderboard for full-duplex voice agents, a competitive space that includes major AI providers.
  • The Starlink deployment provides real-world performance data at scale, with 70% autonomous resolution of customer support cases.
  • Real-time reasoning without added latency is a key differentiator xAI is highlighting, potentially making the model better suited to natural, fast-paced conversations than systems that pause to reason.
