Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0: xAI's Flagship Voice Agent Now on API

xAI has released grok-voice-think-fast-1.0, a voice model optimized for complex, multi-step customer support and sales workflows, with early results from Starlink showing a 20% sales conversion rate.

xAI Blog 2026-04-23 22:25 UTC Key: xai-blog::identity::grok-voice-think-fast-1 Confidence: moderate Mode: claude

Article body

xAI has announced grok-voice-think-fast-1.0, a new flagship voice agent the company describes as its most capable to date. The model is designed for complex, ambiguous, multi-step workflows across customer support, sales, and enterprise applications, and is now accessible via API. It ranks first on the τ-voice Bench leaderboard, which evaluates full-duplex voice agents under realistic conditions including background noise, accents, interruptions, and turn-taking. The model natively supports more than 25 languages.

The company says grok-voice-think-fast-1.0 was developed in close collaboration with Starlink to combine high intelligence with low response latency and natural conversational ability. Key features include precise data entry and read-back — collecting and confirming structured information like email addresses, physical addresses, phone numbers, and account numbers even when spoken quickly or with a heavy accent — as well as real-time reasoning with no added latency, and improved resistance to confident-but-wrong responses.

Starlink is already using the model to power its phone sales and customer support experience at +1 (888) GO STARLINK. xAI reports a 20% conversion rate on sales inquiries and a 70% resolution rate for customer support calls handled autonomously by the agent, which orchestrates 28 distinct tools across hundreds of workflows. The agent autonomously performs hardware troubleshooting, issues replacement hardware, and grants service credits.

Why this matters

  • xAI claims a benchmark top spot with grok-voice-think-fast-1.0 on the τ-voice Bench, a competitive full-duplex voice evaluation.
  • Starlink's live deployment shows meaningful business outcomes — 20% of inbound sales calls converting and 70% of support calls resolved without human intervention.
  • Real-time reasoning without latency trade-offs is a technical distinction the company is leaning on to differentiate from voice agents that default to slow, over-thought responses.
