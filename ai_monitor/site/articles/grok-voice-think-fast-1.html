xAI Makes Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 Available via Public API

xAI has released Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0, its most capable voice agent, as a public API alongside complementary speech-to-text and text-to-speech tools.

xAI Blog 2026-04-23 22:25 UTC Key: xai-blog::identity::grok-voice-think-fast-1 Confidence: moderate Mode: claude

Article body

xAI announced on April 23, 2026, that Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0, its most advanced voice-capable AI agent, is now available to developers through a public API. The release marks xAI's most capable voice agent being offered as a standalone product for external integration. Alongside the voice agent launch, xAI released Grok Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) APIs, enabling developers to build end-to-end voice pipelines using the company's own speech models. The announcement was published on the company's official news page at x.ai, alongside an item covering the new speech APIs.

Why this matters

  • Developers can now integrate xAI's voice agent into third-party applications without relying on third-party voice infrastructure, opening new integration possibilities.
  • The bundled STT and TTS APIs give xAI full control over the voice pipeline from input to output, potentially improving coherence across stages.
  • The move signals xAI's continued expansion beyond text-only AI into real-time multimodal interaction — a space dominated by OpenAI and Google.
