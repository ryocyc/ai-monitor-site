MiniMax has released version 2.6 of its AI music platform, introducing an updated 'Cover' tool that allows users to take any melody and recreate it with new production characteristics. The update page specifically highlights improvements in bass depth and instrumentation richness as the headline changes.

The Cover feature sits alongside a set of genre presets on the platform, including Pop, Pop Melody, Hyperpop, Dance Club Electronic, Drive Sports, Trap, Video Scoring EDM, and Epic Game. Users can access the feature via the platform's API or through a direct web interface, suggesting both a developer-facing and consumer-facing workflow.

For music creators and content producers, the Cover tool offers a way to generate fresh arrangements of melodic ideas without manual production work, which could streamline workflows for social media scoring, game audio, or rapid prototype iteration. The specific improvements to bass and instrumentation quality address two of the most common criticisms of early AI music output.
